This is Rock Paper Scissor Game.

Game Rules

The game is played by two players, and each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. The possible shapes are:

  • Rock: Represented by a fist.z
  • Paper: Represented by an open hand.
  • Scissors: Represented by a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V.

    • Each of these shapes beats or loses to the other in a specific way:

  • Rock crushes Scissors (Rock wins).
  • Scissors cuts Paper (Scissors wins).
  • Paper covers Rock (Paper wins).

    • If both players choose the same shape, the round is a tie.

    How to Play:

    1. Preparation: Two players face each other and count down, saying “Rock, Paper, Scissors, Shoot” or simply “1, 2, 3, Shoot!”
    2. Making a Choice: On "shoot" (or "3"), both players make one of the three hand gestures simultaneously.
    3. Determine the Winner: Based on the rules above, determine the winner of the round:
      • If one player’s hand shape beats the other, they win.
      • If both choose the same shape, it’s a tie, and they go again.
